The yarrow-stalk method (蓍筮) is older than the coins and weights its odds differently: a moving yin appears but 1 time in 16, a moving yang 3. Every stalk here is drawn from noise — no two milfoil quite alike; meet a single one at /yarrow.  ·  g.mino.mobi
