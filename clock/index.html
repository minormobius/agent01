Fractal Geometry

Helix Calendar

A parametric fractal visualization of time. Nested helices at every scale — each zoom level reveals the next layer of coils.

Topology

Level 0 — Year helix — One revolution per year. Color encodes month.

Level 1 — Quarters (×4) — 4 coils per year.

Level 2 — Weeks (×13) — 13 coils per quarter.

Level 3 — Days (×7) — 7 coils per week.

Level 4 — Hours (×24) — 24 coils per day.

Level 5 — 6-min (×10) — 10 blocks per hour.

Level 6 — 30-sec (×12) — 12 ticks per 6-min block.

Tap to focus, scroll/pinch to zoom. Use the tune panel to adjust fractal geometry live. Toggle individual levels on/off.

Reading the time

Brightness encodes time of day — midnight is dimmest, noon is brightest, with dawn/dusk transitions between.

Gold ring pulses at the current moment — tracks your position on the helix in real time.

Controls

Drag to orbit the camera around the helix.

Scroll to zoom in and out.

Technical

Rendered with WebGPU. No frameworks, no build step, no backend. Parametric fractal — each level's geometry is derived from orbitRatio and tubeFrac, tunable in real time.

7 LOD levels with smooth crossfade. Level 0 always loaded; deeper levels generated on-demand around the selected point. Click breadcrumbs to jump between zoom levels.
