How it reads

Between each pair of parentheses runs an invisible line. The table looks along it and counts the distinct dots you've left — odd → • (a single point, active), even → •• (a double point, passive). It never counts for you; it measures the sand. Stack four lines and you have a figure; sixteen lines give the four Mothers, the seed of the whole geomantic shield — which is the next step.
