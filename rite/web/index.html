The graph of where a writer points outward. PageRank over co-occurrence; query to surface a neighborhood.

Pulls a writer's repo, extracts every external link from post facets (skipping bsky.app / *.bsky.social), and builds a co-occurrence graph: two URLs share an edge whenever they appear in the same thread. PageRank ranks the most central links. The search box runs personalized PageRank seeded on URLs whose domain or anchor text matches your query — the top results are the URLs the writer connects most strongly to that idea.

Best for writers who link out a lot. Sparse linkers will see a sparse, less interesting graph.
