A Redactle for Bluesky threads. Pull a handle, pick the longest prose, guess the words.

We pull the user's full repo as a CAR file from their PDS, parse it locally with WASM, and rank their longest pure-prose threads (no images, no link cards). Big repos take ~30 s. Pure client-side; nothing leaves your browser.
