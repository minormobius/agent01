A semantic map of what a Bluesky writer reposts. /ask/ is voice; /signal/ is taste.

We pull the writer's repo, find every app.bsky.feed.repost record, hydrate each target post via app.bsky.feed.getPosts, embed once with Workers AI (BGE), and project to 2D. Each point is something the writer chose to amplify — the cluster they fall into is the taste pocket. Most recent 3000 reposts; self-reposts and image-only targets are dropped.
