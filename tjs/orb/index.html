The shared idea: a spherical kaleidoscope is a Schwarz triangle (p,q,r) with 1/p+1/q+1/r > 1. Solve that over the integers and the complete list is one infinite family (2,2,n) plus exactly three exceptions — tetra (2,3,3), octa (2,3,4), icosa (2,3,5). Both toys fold the whole sphere into one of those triangles; Sketch paints there, Build evaluates math there. Either way the result is symmetric for free.
