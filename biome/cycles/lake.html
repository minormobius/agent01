biome · lake bioengine — grow the protein in the sewage plant

The terrestrial web asks does the loop close?. The lake asks a sharper, dual-purpose question: can one body of water be both the crew's fish farm and its water-treatment plant? In a closed cylinder the cheapest design makes them the same ecosystem — the fish you eat are grown on the nutrients you'd otherwise strip out with hardware (Todd's eco-machines; integrated poly-culture; constructed treatment wetlands). Two figures of merit: surplus harvestable fish and effective water treatment. Every species is picked for its role in those.
