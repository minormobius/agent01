biome · stability lab — will this web survive?

The box-model dashboard shows what happens over 600 days. This reads the fate of the steady state straight from its linearization — the community matrix (how each species' growth responds to every species' biomass at equilibrium). Its eigenvalues decide stability (May 1972); its symmetric part decides reactivity (Neubert & Caswell 1997); its inverse ranks keystones (Bender 1984). No 600-day run — the verdict is analytic.
