myology lab — build a muscular system, solve its stability

Draw muscles onto the skeleton and get a live structural-stability solve: every muscle only pulls, each makes torque = force × moment arm, and the body stands only when every joint is held (capacity to cancel its buckling torque) and antagonised, with the centre of mass over the support. Add muscles by hand, or let the solver grow a whole musculature — vertebrae and all — from first principles.
