what the circle is showing

One steady state. Energy: the light flux over the floor circumference must leave as εσT⁴ over the (larger) skin circumference — that single balance fixes the radiator temperature, and the reservoir/floor sit above it by the heat-pipe and contact ΔTs (heat flows outward, so the floor is always the warm end). Temperature hangs a dry centrifugal adiabat (cp·T+Φ=const, Φ=−½ω²r²) off the floor and adds a radiative inversion that warms the axis — crank it and "up" flips cold→hot. Pressure is centrifugal hydrostatic balance dP/dr=ρω²r with the local temperature. Humidity hangs off the floor; with the jets off it stratifies and traps under the inversion, with them on the fountain well-mixes it (conserving the water); fog is wherever RH≥1. Wind is a convective scale (B·z_i)^⅓ choked by the inversion's stability, plus the jet sheet, in a frame where Coriolis (f=2ω) dominates (Rossby ≪ 1). The inner rim carries the ratchet topography — asymmetric teeth that let lakes (constant-radius arcs) sit at all.
