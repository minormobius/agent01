Item Sprites

A procedural, deterministic item engine. A seed rolls a kind × material × quality × affixes item — appraised by an oracle into a worth and a grade — and drawn as a 32×32 sprite, no AI, no network. Same seed, same item, for ever (so ?n= is a permalink). The catalog mirrors hoop's econ deck → roll → oracle → genome arc; hoards are the item-genome groundwork. Built to feed /hoop.

The hoard

How it works. items.js is the characteristics engine (the deck of KINDS, MATERIALS, QUALITY, AFFIXES + rollItem + the scoreItem oracle + rollGenome); sprite.js draws each kind's silhouette tinted by material, framed by rarity, decorated by affix. Both are pure and node-tested (test/*.selftest.mjs). Next: per-kind silhouette genes, mutation, and lineage — item genomes.
