Isopod Lab

The body plan that lives between two others. An isopod takes the segmented armoured body from the axial plan and grows one leg pair per segment from the polypod — walked by the same metachronal wave. It's the proof that axial and polypod aren't separate: they're a segmented spine, ± a leg per segment.
