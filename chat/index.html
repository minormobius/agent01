How a session works
  1. Pick a partner (difficulty) and a scenario (party, coffee, new colleague…).
  2. Converse in real time — text now, voice coming. The partner stays in character.
  3. End the session and get a scored breakdown: per-axis score, the moment that earned it, and one tip each.
  4. Watch your title climb from Finding your feet to Magnetic as the axes even out.

Roadmap: voice mode (speak instead of type), then real-time multiplayer — 2–4 humans, a lobby, and peer-rated exit surveys feeding a scoreboard. Robo mode is the training ground that gets built first.
