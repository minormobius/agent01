Room · work in progress

This is where a permalinked lobby will live (/room/<roomId>): join over WebRTC, hear everyone live, and — the part that makes it a studio — record your own microphone locally at full fidelity while the call audio is just for monitoring.

What gets built here

  1. WebRTC mesh lobby (own room coordinator, modeled on the existing audio/ Durable Object).
  2. A second high-quality MediaRecorder on your own mic — the keeper track (the "double-ender").
  3. A shared recording epoch broadcast to every participant so tracks align later.
  4. Chunked upload of your local track to your PDS as com.minomobi.podcast.track blobs.
  5. A com.minomobi.podcast.session manifest tying the room's tracks together for /prod.

Lexicons are scaffolded in pod/lexicons/. Pipeline notes in pod/README.md.
