How it works

There is no central catalogue here, on purpose. Record in /room, edit in /prod, and publish — the episode and its audio blobs are written to your own PDS. Your feed at /u/<handle>/feed.xml is built live from your repo, so it updates the instant you publish. The only thing this service does is construct that RSS and stream your blobs. What you publish, and whether you publish, is yours.

Built on the same ATProto plumbing as airchat and the audio rooms — recordings, episodes and subscriptions all live as records + chunked blobs on each user's own PDS. Architecture notes in pod/README.md.
