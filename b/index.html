atmosphere ✦

the Bluesky corner of mino.mobi

Every tool here lives on ATProto — the protocol behind Bluesky. They read the public network, map its graph, dissect a handle's posts, or write records straight to your own PDS. No backend servers, no SaaS: static files on Cloudflare Pages, data on the open protocol. Identity, when needed, runs through one shared OAuth worker.

Social apps — your own thing on ATProto12

These write records: votes, voice posts, answers, edits. Sign in with Bluesky; the data lands on a PDS, not our servers.

  • pollwritesblind sigs
    Anonymous polling with RSA blind signatures — the host can't link your identity to your vote. Public like-based mode too.
  • airchatwritesvoice
    Voice-first social on ATProto. Browser records, Whisper transcribes, the audio blob lives on your PDS.
  • answerswritesQ&A
    Ask anything, answered by the ATmosphere — questions, answers, votes and best-answer selection as ATProto records.
  • ritewritesprose
    Sentence-editing drill plus eight surfaces over Bluesky prose. The hub for the eight sub-tools below.
  • fodderrite
    Tinder-style swipe deck crowdsourcing new corpus sentences mined from Project Gutenberg.
  • redactrite
    Redactle over a Bluesky user's longest prose threads — guess the censored words.
  • askrite
    Semantic search over a profile's prose threads, mapped in 2D by embedding.
  • atlasrite
    Multi-view analytics over a writer's threads — length, difficulty, Flesch, Pareto.
  • lexiconrite
    Word-level lenses — emotion, concreteness, sentiment, distinctiveness — over a profile's vocabulary.
  • listrite
    Semantic themes across a whole Bluesky list, aggregated from each member's clusters.
  • webrite
    Outbound-link knowledge graph from a writer's posts — PageRank over co-occurring URLs.
  • signalrite
    A semantic map of what a writer reposts — their taste, as distinct from their voice.
Network & community — graph structure5

The follow graph as terrain: communities, your tightest circle, who to meet next.

  • zoomcanvas
    SimCluster community viewer — infinite-canvas map of feed communities with hex-packed avatars and bridge arcs.
  • biskdigest
    The SimCluster Daily — a deterministic morning digest of a Bluesky neighborhood: top posts, deepest thread, sentiment weather.
  • clustergraph
    Find your tightest circle — the largest group of your follows who all mutually follow each other.
  • seekdiscovery
    Find a friend — follows-of-follows you don't yet follow, ranked by network density.
  • diskhyperbolic
    Poincaré interaction map — a hyperbolic-disk view of account interactions, more room at the edges.
Account & post analysis — point a handle, get insight12

Embeddings and aggregates over a profile's public posts. Read-only; nothing is written.

  • empathytimeline
    See Bluesky as anyone sees it — type a handle, get their reconstructed timeline, network, and blocks.
  • judgeembeddings
    Posting profile — personality traits and valence from post embeddings, topics via k-means.
  • noveltydrift
    Semantic novelty trajectory — how an account's posting drifts or repeats over time.
  • ternarytemperament
    Three lusts, one chart — a ternary plot of posting temperament: flesh, knowledge, argument.
  • echocompare
    Post density head to head — two handles side by side on volume and patterns.
  • sparktwo handles
    What they liked before they followed — every post a follower had liked before the follow, a hint at what won them over.
  • dyadtwo handles
    A dyadline — every recorded interaction between two accounts (likes, reposts, replies, quotes, mentions, follows, blocks) on one timeline, both directions.
  • gcblock checker
    Who blocks whom — paste a set of accounts and a set of posters, get a matrix of which accounts directly block which posters. Public block records, no login.
  • gc · mutuals who blocktwo handles
    Mutuals of X who block Y — X's mutual follows (or all followers) that block a third named party, off public follow + block records.
  • gc · who blocks themone handle
    Everyone who blocks a named user — the full count + list, from the firehose-built block index. Also a clean JSON API for agents.
  • densitybrevity
    Post brevity, measured — filter posts by exact word count, ranked by character length.
  • wildgroup
    Group novelty — how wild is your corner? Semantic novelty across a whole list against the group centroid.

Plumbing — the shared ATProto layerdev

What the surfaces above are built on. Not pages you visit — the workers, libraries, and pipelines that make the rest possible.

  • auth.mino.mobiworkers/auth
    Shared OAuth BFF — PKCE + DPoP + PAR + private_key_jwt. Holds the confidential client; every site signs in through it and proxies PDS calls via /pds/*.
  • feed.mino.mobiworkers/feed
    SimCluster feed generator. Community detection on the mutual-follow graph every 6h; serves getFeedSkeleton + the graph data behind zoom & bisk.
  • scores.mino.mobiworkers/scores
    Generic multi-game leaderboard. Identity delegated to the auth worker; any game submits with no worker change.
  • bsky-botworkers/bsky-bot
    Notification listener — polls listNotifications every 5 min, dispatches mention handlers.
  • packages/atprotopds · bsky · crypto
    The shared library: identity resolution + authenticated PDS ops, read-only public-API wrappers, and ECDH+AES vault crypto.
  • packages/oauth-clientauth.js
    Browser-side AuthClient — login, session, and a PDS proxy. No build step; what new static sites import.
  • rite/lib/atprotoCAR + WASM
    Browser-side repo-export parsing — pulls a writer's full CAR and parses it with a Rust→WASM reader for the rite prose tools.
  • post-to-blueskypipeline
    Pushes time/posts/*.md as threaded Bluesky posts across three accounts (main, modulo, morphyx).
  • register-feed-generatorpipeline
    One-time registration of the SimCluster feed generators on Bluesky.
  • publishersphylo · answers · whtwnd · cards
    Pipelines that write com.minomobi.* and com.whtwnd.* records to a curator PDS from repo data.
