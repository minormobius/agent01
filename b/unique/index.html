unique

phrases only you have ever posted

Give a handle. This finds every two- and three-word phrase they've used exactly once that no one else on all of Bluesky has ever posted — their hapax legomena, the fingerprints of a voice. Repo is harvested at the edge; only the phrases they never repeated themselves are checked against platform-wide search, in parallel, streamed back as they land.

    How it works. A phrase can only be globally unique if its author used it exactly once — anything they repeated is already ≥2 uses on the network. That free pre-filter throws away the bulk of the n-grams with zero queries; only the survivors hit search. Trigrams are far likelier to be unique than bigrams, so they're the default. "Unique" means exactly one indexed post contains the phrase; the search index lags and honours opt-outs, so treat it as a strong hint, not a proof. Read-only public data · part of b.mino.mobi.
