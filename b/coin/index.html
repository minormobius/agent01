post only what no one has ever posted

A posting box with one rule: you can only send it if it's new. As you type, every two- and three-word phrase is checked against all of Bluesky. The send button stays locked until your draft carries at least one phrase no one has ever posted — and the instant you post it, that phrase is yours alone: a fresh hapax. Sign in with Bluesky to send; the novelty check itself needs no login.

How it works. Your draft is split into contiguous two- and three-word phrases (never across a sentence end or line break), and each is run through Bluesky's full-text search from the server. A phrase is novel when zero indexed posts contain it. One novel phrase is enough to unlock send. Sending writes a normal app.bsky.feed.post to your PDS via ATProto OAuth, scoped to publishing posts only — nothing else. The search index lags and honours opt-outs, so "novel" is a strong hint, not a proof. Read-only checks · sibling of the unique finder · part of b.mino.mobi.
