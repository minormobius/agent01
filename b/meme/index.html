Personal memes

Phrases they repeat that turned up on no other account. The more times they've said it, the stronger the signature.
    No phrase they repeat is unique to them — everything they say a lot, others say too.

    Co-memeticists

    Accounts who echo the same repeated phrases — ranked by how many they share. The niche the phrase, the tighter the tie.
      No one else shares the phrases they repeat.
      How it works. Only the bigrams/trigrams the account used at least the chosen number of times survive the free pre-filter — those are the candidates. Each is run through Bluesky search: zero other authors ⇒ a personal meme; a handful of others ⇒ co-memeticists; a saturated result window ⇒ ordinary language, dropped. The search index lags, honours opt-outs, and returns a bounded window, so a very prolific author's own posts can crowd out a rare sharer — treat this as a strong hint, not a proof.
