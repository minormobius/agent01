Garden Plot

The substrate under the microscope — soil grain, moisture and desiccation cracks (the sand engine) with the flora grown on it, roots in the earth below, each plant coloured by its Galenic temperament. Every plant is a pure function of a seed, so an NPC's garden reproduces exactly. Click a plant chip to swap it; drag the sliders to work the soil.
