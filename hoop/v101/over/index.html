The Overworld — roam the grow-deck

The ship's outer grow-deck, walked. You're the gold-rimmed figure; the ecology grows wild around you across a voronoi of terrain bands (dry heath, sunlit meadow, orchard grove, deep thicket, reed fen, open water you can't cross). The world streams in chunks as you move — roam out and back and it's the same landscape, deterministic from the seed. Two verbs up here: gather a herb (into your pantry, to brew at a bench) and face a bee-swarm or spider. Same plants the garden grows, each keeping the soul of its growth-form.
