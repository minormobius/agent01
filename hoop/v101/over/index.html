The Overworld

The ship's outer grow-deck — the whole curated ecology grown wild, not in beds. A moisture-and-elevation field carves coherent terrain (dry heath, sunlit meadow, orchard grove, deep thicket, reed fen, open water); every band grows its own slice of the palette. These are the same plants the garden fixture grows, drawn here as silhouettes that keep the soul of each — a broadleaf towers, a fennel is feathery, an aromatic is a grey mound, a reed is blue blades. Deterministic from a seed, so an NPC's overworld reproduces exactly. Drag to pan, scroll to zoom.

the canonical farm is seed 21 — the closing food-web the viability oracle scores. every other seed is a fresh draw from the same palette.
