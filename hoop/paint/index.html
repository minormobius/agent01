the idea

Seed the floor plan's membranes (the walls) with Voronoi nuclei at wall spacing, so walls are fine and crisp — thickness ≈ wall spacing. The interiors don't need that detail, so the floor nuclei are density-graded: a big seed at each room centre, fining toward the walls (room spacing sets how coarse the middle gets). The cells fit between the two. Paint the Voronoi of them all: wall cells = wall, the rest = floor. Doors are two-nuclei-wide gaps cut in the wall and bridged with floor; a spanning tree of them keeps every room connected, and loops adds extra doors — generated roads. Zones force higher-order structure: rooms agglomerate into sized super-regions (a housing unit, a hospital) — dense doors inside a zone, sparse arterials between them.

Desire-line roads grow streets the naturalistic way: place attractors (homes, work, the daily basket), route the trips, and let conductance follow flux. Roads are the superlevel set of the traffic field — the chambers the most journeys cross become open concourse (zero walls); the rest stay sequestered buildings with one door each. That traffic field is the Laplace transform of NPC motion, so the same solve gives the streets and the foot-traffic glow at once. μ dials grid (sublinear, many parallel streets) ↔ tree (superlinear, one arterial).
