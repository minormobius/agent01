the idea

Take the floor plan's membranes (the walls between rooms) and seed them with Voronoi nuclei spaced s. Hold the floor nuclei a clear band of ~s/2 off the walls, then paint the Voronoi of all of them. Wall-seeded cells = the wall; the rest = floor. Because the floor is held off by ~s/2, wall thickness ≈ s — so the one knob, nucleus spacing, directly sets the wall.
