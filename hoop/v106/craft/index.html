The Smithy

The make verb for equipment — the alchemy bench's harder-metal cousin. Where the bench turns garden reagents into draughts, the smithy turns the ship's production commodities into gear: choose a body-plan, a material, an era of tech and how much care, and forge a genome item. Materials map to the forge's seven commodities (organic↔biomass, metal↔metal, exotic↔trace, the scarce keystone), so a craft spends real stock. Tech is story-gated — your rank sets the era ceiling (salvage → ship-grade); dismantling unwanted loot recovers a fraction of its commodities to forge with. This is the design testbed before the make-room bench.
