The unified alphabet

One language for body & craft:
☉ ☽ ☿ ♀ ♂ ♃ ♄

Every character, reagent, ingot, gem, skill and foe speaks two words — a faction (the body it wears) and a planet (the flavor it carries). Three bodies, seven flavors, twenty-one identities.

Two axes, read together

The faction is the body. The planet is the flavor.

The old strawman hung the FLESH·CHASSIS·ANIMA triad on the planets — and five of seven came out ANIMA-heavy, because most civic verbs lean soul-ward. Moving the triad onto the three factions makes it a clean split, and frees the seven planets to be a second, orthogonal axis: the register a thing is cast in.

Faction → Body

Your stat spine and your craft/combat school. One of three.

Planet → Flavor

Your metal, colour, element, and combat matchup. One of seven.

3 × 7 = 21

The identity grid

Character creation is a single pick in this matrix — one cell, one species. Pick a row for how you're built, a column for what you're made of. Select a cell to inspect the identity.

One grammar, five screens

How every vertical speaks it

Each make-verb becomes the same demo-tier workflow: planetary inputs → a planetary output, with your faction setting the school. Less choice, one palette, one set of glyphs.

Combat

The rulership wheel

Two layers, both already balanced. The planet sets a seven-way rock-paper-scissors — in the Chaldean cycle each planet has the edge over the three that follow and yields to the three before (every planet beats exactly three). The faction body sets the school beneath it: FLESH endures and mends, CHASSIS strikes and soaks, ANIMA spends flux. Element × school — the same two words.

What we're moving off

The strawman's derived leans, when the triad rode the planets — the skew that prompted this:

Confirmed & in code — all five verticals wired. The model lives in hoop/v106/planets.js (node-tested): faction → body (continuant·FLESH / rindwalker·CHASSIS / drift·ANIMA, leans derived from each faction's verbs), and the seven planets as a free second axis — any faction × any planet, all 21 reachable. Every vertical now speaks it: creation picks a verb (→ body), leaving faction to the threshold fork and planetary flavor to be grown — a radar tallied from every planet-aligned thing you touch, whose dominant axis becomes your combat register; forge stamps a metal's planet register (materialPlanet); brew carries a reagent's canonical planetKey; gems resonate with the wielder's body; combat reads the 7-way heptagram as an element-over-element damage multiplier (elementMult). Colours and per-cell flavor stay tunable; the structure is settled.
