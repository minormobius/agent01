the ship at a glance

cross-section — the whole world, perpendicular to the axis

The sun-line runs down the centre. Gravity points outward (toward the hull) and grows with radius: zero at the axis, full at the rim. Most of the cylinder is open sky — the livable rind is the thin band at the edge.

the play-slice — the (axial × radial) plane we'll actually generate

Brighter toward the core (the sun-line); heavier toward the hull. Compartments are big pressure volumes behind thick bulkheads (the "void is structure"); hatches make a breach local. This strip is what the dungeon-crawl streams.
