the flag spine

load-bearing NPC deck quests · the deck-stacking guarantee — hoop story engine, v101

One load-bearing NPC per deck blocks advancement to the next deck. They give an informational quest: “gather lore until these flags are set.” The flags are set by the lore you gather — a fragment may carry a flag, set the moment you crystallize it. When every prerequisite flag is held the quest is ripe, the NPC pages you, and a previously hidden, flag-gated turn-in dialogue opens and advances the deck. The engine guarantees you can never draw forever without the fragments — that's the demo at the bottom.
