The Book of Sand is generated, not authored: every page number yields the same tale for ever, so a teller can post its mythograph to the Tabard before it speaks. The apparatus — story-graph, motif index, character web and the synergistic mythograph — is the one built for the annotated medieval tales at read.mino.mobi/pendragon, run forward here as a generator instead of backward as analysis. After Borges, The Book of Sand: a book with no first page and no last.
