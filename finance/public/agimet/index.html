College unemployment premium · HS-only minus BA+ computed, percentage points

A positive number means non-college workers face higher unemployment than college workers. Historically positive throughout the series; spikes during recessions (the gap widens because HS-only workers are laid off first); narrows in tight labor markets.

Series: UNRATE (overall), LNS14027659 (BA+), LNS14027689 (HS only), USREC (NBER recessions).
