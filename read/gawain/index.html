A working translation and analysis of the Pearl-Poet's masterpiece. The English is an original translation; the Middle English beside it is a lightly normalised reading text after Richard Morris's 1864 edition of the Cotton Nero A.x manuscript (Project Gutenberg #14568, public domain). The Propp mapping is interpretive. Character cards, motif index, mythograph and storybook to follow, mirroring the apparatus built for Culhwch ac Olwen. This site sits under the broader Arthurian historiography at read.mino.mobi/pendragon.
