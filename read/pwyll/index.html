A deep read of the foundational Welsh prose tale. The Middle Welsh source is a CC BY-SA 4.0 reading text from the Welsh Wikisource, descending from the medieval manuscripts (White Book of Rhydderch / Red Book of Hergest); the original English translation runs alongside it movement by movement, with character cards, character web, story graph, motif index, mythograph and a faithful middle-grade storybook retelling — the same apparatus built for Sir Gawain and the Green Knight, Culhwch ac Olwen, and Sir Orfeo. This site sits alongside the broader Arthurian and Brittonic historiography at read.mino.mobi/pendragon, where the cross-tale comparative layer (motifs, Propp functions, character archetypes across all the annotated tales) now lives.
