Working scaffold for a deep read of the foundational Welsh prose tale. The Middle Welsh source is a CC BY-SA 4.0 reading text from the Welsh Wikisource, descending from the medieval manuscripts (White Book of Rhydderch / Red Book of Hergest); an original English translation will be written alongside it movement by movement in subsequent passes — character cards, story graph, motif index, mythograph and storybook to follow, mirroring the apparatus built for Sir Gawain and the Green Knight, Culhwch ac Olwen, and Sir Orfeo. This site sits alongside the broader Arthurian and Brittonic historiography at read.mino.mobi/pendragon, where the cross-tale comparative layer (motifs, Propp functions, character archetypes across all the annotated tales) now lives.
