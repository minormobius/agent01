Working scaffold for a deep read of the medieval English Orpheus. The Middle English source (Martha Hale Shackford's 1913 edition, after David Laing 1884, ultimately from the Auchinleck Manuscript NLS Advocates 19.2.1, c. 1330) is in place; an original English translation will be written alongside it in subsequent passes — character cards, story graph, motif index, mythograph and storybook to follow, mirroring the apparatus built for Sir Gawain and the Green Knight and Culhwch ac Olwen. This site sits alongside the broader Arthurian and post-classical historiography at read.mino.mobi/pendragon.
