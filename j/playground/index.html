What you're seeing

  • The sphere starts empty; everything outside its surface is a reservoir at the surface concentration.
  • Explicit finite-difference Fickian diffusion runs on the GPU; the front obeys the same √(Dt) law you fit on the Model page.
  • First-order reaction k consumes the species inside, giving a reaction–diffusion (Thiele) steady state instead of full saturation.
