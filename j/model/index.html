      What power law should this follow?

      Fickian diffusion into a sphere held at fixed surface concentration:
      φ(ρ,τ) = 1 + (2/πρ)·Σ (−1)ⁿ/n · sin(nπρ)·e^(−n²π²τ)
      Total uptake:
      Mₜ/M∞ = 1 − (6/π²)·Σ (1/n²)·e^(−n²π²τ) (τ = Dt/R²)
      • Early-time power law: Mₜ/M∞ ≈ (6/√π)·√(Dt/R²) − 3Dt/R² → ∝ t^½.
      • Korsmeyer–Peppas Mₜ/M∞ = k·tⁿ: a sphere's Fickian exponent is n ≈ 0.43 (slab 0.50, cylinder 0.45).
      • Penetration depth grows as λ ≈ √(Dt) — the semi-empirical exp model captures this directly.
