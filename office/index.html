Scroll to fall into a poster. Each is another office. how it works
Every desk is a page of mino.mobi; the wall behind it is papered with posters of its sub-pages, each a smaller office with its own wall — all the way down.
• Scroll / pinch to zoom straight through the walls.
• Click a poster to step into that office.
• Click the glowing monitor (or double-click anything) to open the live site.
• Breadcrumb or Esc to step back out; H for the lobby.
