how it works
A list of ~750 ubiquitously famous cultural works is embedded with all-MiniLM-L6-v2 (384-dim) in your browser. Your query is embedded the same way. Then we run matching pursuit: pick the basis vector most aligned with the query (positive or negative), subtract its contribution, repeat. The result is a short signed combination of famous works whose semantic vectors best approximate yours. The cosine fit reports how close the reconstructed vector is to the query.
