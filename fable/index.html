games no one wrote

Six puzzle-spaces, one discipline. Each takes a vast combinatorial space, generates it deterministically from a seed, and sets an oracle over it — a real solver that certifies every artifact: solvable, fair, graded. Nothing here was authored; everything here was proved. The machine below is the thesis running: it rolls a tiny world in each wing's form, floods it with the oracle's search, and inks the answer in gold. Forever.

The ladder — what each wing brokeVIII

One experiment, climbing. Every rung keeps the oracle and breaks a form.

  • puzzbroke nothing — static deduction on a grid. The solver certifies a unique solution and proves no guessing is needed.
  • knackbroke stillness — time. Discrete moves, composable mechanics; a BFS finds the optimal answer to every level.
  • fluxbroke the grid — continuous space, real physics. The solver sweeps every possible launch and maps which ones win.
  • gyrebroke the boundary — a closed curved surface. Geodesics on a torus; the winning shot's winding numbers are part of the grade.
  • morphbroke the game itself — a generator of generators. Each roll samples a whole grammar: topology, law, goal, look. One oracle certifies them all.
  • driftbroke the board — the substrate is meaning. Words wired by embedding; crossings and groupings certified on the semantic graph.
  • forgebroke the rules — a foundry that mints laws no one wrote, fingerprints each by behavior, and keeps only the ones measurably new and certifiably playable.
  • dealbroke solitude — card games with hidden hands and an adversary. No more "the answer": a tribunal of simulated opponents certifies each game terminates, rewards skill, and plays fair.
The doctrine — three rules, never broken3

Seeded

Page n yields the identical artifact on every machine, for ever (xmur3 → mulberry32, the house pair). Determinism is what makes a permalink mean something — and what makes an inexhaustible space navigable rather than a slot machine.

Certified

Every artifact ships with a verifiable answer. Uniqueness by exhaustive search, optimal par by BFS, winning basins by sweeping the whole action space, separability by measured margin. Unsolvable rolls die at birth. The oracle vouches or nothing ships.

Graded

Difficulty is earned, not asserted — read off the techniques the solution forces, the size of the search, the precision of the window, the winding of the path. An interest battery ranks the space, so “surprise me” lands somewhere worth a second look.
